Privacy Policy — Your Data Stays Yours

Last updated: November 2025

1. We Collect Almost Nothing

NOUFI is designed from the ground up to respect your privacy. We do not require any account, login, or personal information to play.

The only data that ever leaves your phone is related to ads (via Google AdMob).
Everything else — scores, teams, questions — stays 100% on your device.

2. Local Data (Never Sent Anywhere)

We store the following only on your phone:

  • Team names, avatars, and scores
  • Game settings and progress
  • Downloaded trivia questions (using secure local storage)

This data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the app.

3. Advertising (Google AdMob)

To keep NOUFI free, we show non-intrusive banner and interstitial ads powered by Google AdMob.

AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Advertising ID (resettable in your device settings)
  • Device model and OS version
  • IP address and general location (country level)
  • Ad interaction data

Google’s privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy

You can disable personalized ads anytime: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

4. Fully Offline After First Launch

On first launch, NOUFI downloads all questions and saves them locally. After that, the game works completely offline — no internet or data collection required.

5. Third-Party Services

The only third-party service we use is Google AdMob for advertisements. We do not use analytics, crash reporting, or any other tracking tools.

6. Data Security

All local data is stored securely on your device using encrypted storage. We never have access to your gameplay data.

7. Your Rights

You can:

  • Delete all data → Uninstall the app
  • Reset your advertising ID in Android settings
  • Disable personalized ads in your device settings

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy occasionally. The latest version will always be available at this link.
