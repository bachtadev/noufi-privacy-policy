NOUFI

Privacy Policy — We collect almost nothing

Last updated: November 2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to NOUFI — the fun, offline-first multiplayer trivia game!

We respect your privacy and designed the entire app so that you can play without ever giving us personal information.

2. We Do NOT Collect Personal Data

NOUFI does not require any account, login, or sign-up.

We never collect or store:

  • Your name, email, or phone number
  • Location, contacts, photos, or files
  • Any personally identifiable information

3. What We Do Collect (Very Little)

Local data only (on your phone):

  • Team names, avatars, scores, selected themes
  • Downloaded trivia questions (stored safely with Hive)

All of this stays on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the app.

Ads (Google AdMob):

We show a few non-intrusive ads to keep the game free. Google AdMob may automatically collect:

  • Advertising ID (you can reset it anytime)
  • Device type & OS version
  • Ad interaction data

Google’s privacy policy applies: policies.google.com/privacy

You can turn off personalized ads in your phone settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization.

4. Fully Offline After First Launch

The first time you open NOUFI, we download all questions and save them locally. After that the game works 100% offline — no internet needed, no data sent.

5. Children’s Privacy

NOUFI is family-friendly and safe for all ages. Because we collect zero personal information, we fully comply with COPPA and children’s privacy laws worldwide.

6. Security & Your Rights

All your game data is stored securely on your device only. You can:

  • Delete everything → just uninstall the app
  • Reset your advertising ID in Android settings
  • Opt out of personalized ads anytime
